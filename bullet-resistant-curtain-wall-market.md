<p><p>The bullet-resistant curtain wall market features two primary types: unitized curtain walls and stick curtain walls. Unitized curtain walls are pre-fabricated panels assembled in a factory and installed as large sections, offering rapid installation and enhanced quality control. Both systems provide essential security features, making them suitable for high-risk environments while contributing to architectural aesthetics and energy efficiency.</p></p>
