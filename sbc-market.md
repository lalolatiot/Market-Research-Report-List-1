<p><p>The SBC market comprises various types of block copolymers, including Styrenic-Butadiene-Styrenic (SBS), Hydrogenated Styrenic Block Copolymers (HSBC), and Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS). SBS is known for its excellent elasticity and strong adhesive properties, commonly used in adhesives and sealants. HSBC offers improved thermal stability and resistance to environmental degradation, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. SIS is valued for its flexibility and clarity, often utilized in personal care products and flexible packaging. Together, these materials cater to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
