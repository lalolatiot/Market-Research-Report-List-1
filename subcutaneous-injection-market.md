<p><p>Subcutaneous injection is a medical technique in which medication is delivered into the fatty tissue layer beneath the skin. This method is commonly used for administering vaccines, insulin, biologics, and certain medications that require slow absorption into the bloodstream. Its advantages include ease of administration, reduced pain compared to intramuscular injections, and the potential for self-administration, making it a preferred choice for chronic disease management.</p><p>The Subcutaneous Injection Market is experiencing significant growth, with factors such as rising incidences of chronic diseases, the increasing prevalence of diabetes, and the growing demand for biologics contributing to this trend. Innovations in injection devices, including prefilled syringes and auto-injectors, are enhancing user convenience and safety. Furthermore, the shift towards home healthcare and self-administration models is driving market expansion.</p><p>The Subcutaneous Injection Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.4% during the forecast period.
