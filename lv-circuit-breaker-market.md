<p><strong>The LV Circuit Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Miniature Circuit Breaker (MCB)</li><li>Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB)</li><li>Air Circuit Breaker (ACB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LV circuit breaker market comprises three key types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB), Moulded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB), and Air Circuit Breakers (ACB). MCBs are designed for low voltage applications, providing protection against overloads and short circuits in residential and commercial settings. MCCBs handle higher current ratings, suitable for industrial applications, offering adjustable trip settings and protection features. ACBs are used for high power applications, providing reliable protection in substations and large industrial environments, with the ability to interrupt large fault currents.</p></p>
