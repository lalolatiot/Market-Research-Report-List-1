Hydrocracking catalysts facilitate the breakdown of heavy oils into lighter fuels. Desulfurization catalysts target sulfur removal specifically, ensuring compliance with environmental regulations. Other catalysts encompass various specialized applications within refining and petrochemical production, optimizing yield and product quality across the market.</p></p>
