<p><p>The steel bar market primarily consists of two types: deformed steel bars and mild steel bars. Deformed steel bars, characterized by their surface ridges, provide enhanced grip in concrete, making them ideal for reinforced structures. Mild steel bars, with a lower carbon content, are softer and more ductile, suitable for applications requiring malleability and weldability. Both types play crucial roles in construction and infrastructure, catering to diverse engineering needs and project specifications, thereby driving demand in the market.</p></p>
