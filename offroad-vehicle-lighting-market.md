<p><p>The off-road vehicle lighting market includes several types, each catering to specific needs. LED lighting is known for its energy efficiency, long lifespan, and bright output, making it popular for rugged terrain. Halogen lighting offers affordability and good illumination but has a shorter lifespan and higher heat emission. HID lighting provides intense brightness and better visibility in dark conditions, but it requires a longer warm-up time and can be costlier. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing off-road driving safety and performance.</p></p>
