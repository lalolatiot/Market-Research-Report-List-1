<p><p>The photoacoustic imaging system market primarily comprises two types: Photoacoustic Tomography Systems and Photoacoustic Microscopy Systems. </p><p>Photoacoustic Tomography Systems use focused laser beams to generate 3D images of tissues by detecting sound waves produced as light interacts with biological samples. Conversely, Photoacoustic Microscopy Systems offer high-resolution imaging at a microscopic level, enabling detailed visualization of cellular structures. Both systems leverage the combination of optical and ultrasound technologies for enhanced imaging, making them valuable for biomedical research and clinical applications.</p></p>
