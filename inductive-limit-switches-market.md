<p><p>Inductive limit switches are crucial in automation, distinguishing between single and multiple types based on application needs. Single inductive limit switches operate independently, detecting the presence or absence of objects within a specific range. In contrast, multiple inductive limit switches allow for the monitoring of several positions or objects simultaneously, enhancing efficiency in systems with complex movement. Both types provide reliable contactless detection, ensuring better safety and precision in industrial environments, making them essential in automation and control applications.</p></p>
