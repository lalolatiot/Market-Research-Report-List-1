<p><p>Reporting tools can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises tools require installation and maintenance on a company's own infrastructure, providing control over data security and customization but often involving higher upfront costs and ongoing IT support. Cloud-based reporting tools, conversely, are hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet. They offer scalability, automatic updates, and reduced infrastructure costs, making them ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and ease of use in data reporting.</p></p>
