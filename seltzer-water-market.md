<p><p>The seltzer water market comprises several distinct types: Artesian seltzer is sourced from a confined aquifer, known for its purity and unique mineral profile. Distilled seltzer undergoes vaporization and condensation, removing impurities and minerals for a clean taste. Mineral seltzer retains naturally occurring minerals that enhance flavor and provide health benefits. Tonic water, often confused with seltzer, includes added flavors and quinine, giving it a distinct taste, commonly used in cocktails. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences.</p></p>
