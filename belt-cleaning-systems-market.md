<p><p>Belt cleaning systems are essential for maintaining conveyor efficiency by removing material build-up on belts. The market primarily comprises two types: scraper systems and brush systems. Scraper systems utilize rigid blades to dislodge and remove residual material, making them effective for tough applications. In contrast, brush systems employ bristles to gently sweep away debris without damaging the belt surface, ideal for sensitive materials. Both systems enhance operational efficiency, reduce maintenance costs, and prevent contamination in various industries.</p></p>
