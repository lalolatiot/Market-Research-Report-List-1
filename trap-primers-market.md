<p><p>Trap primers are devices used to maintain water levels in traps, preventing sewer gases from entering buildings. The market types include:</p><p>1. **Continuous Flow**: These systems provide a steady water supply to traps, ensuring consistent operation.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Pressure Drop Activated**: They activate when a pressure loss occurs, releasing water into the trap.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Flush Valve Operated**: These primers release water during flushing cycles, replenishing trap seals.</p><p>  </p><p>4. **Electronic**: Using sensors, these systems detect when water is needed and activate accordingly, optimizing performance.</p></p>
