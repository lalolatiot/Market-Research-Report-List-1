<p><p>The medical high-temperature sterilizer market comprises several types, primarily steam sterilizers and dry sterilizers, along with other specialized options. Steam sterilizers utilize saturated steam under pressure to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for heat-resistant instruments. Dry sterilizers, on the other hand, use hot air to achieve sterilization, suitable for materials that cannot withstand moisture. Other types may include chemical or radiation methods. Each type addresses specific needs in medical environments, ensuring safe and effective sterilization of equipment.</p></p>
