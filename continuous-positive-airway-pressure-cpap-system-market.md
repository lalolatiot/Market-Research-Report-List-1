<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) system market is divided into two main types: Automatic CPAP and Non-automatic CPAP. Automatic CPAP systems adjust airway pressure automatically based on detected breathing patterns, providing personalized treatment for users. In contrast, Non-automatic CPAP systems deliver a fixed pressure, requiring users to set the pressure manually. Both types cater to individuals with sleep apnea, but automatic models offer enhanced comfort and adaptability, while non-automatic models are typically simpler and more cost-effective.</p></p>
