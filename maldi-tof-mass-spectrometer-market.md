<p><p>MALDI-TOF (Matrix-Assisted Laser Desorption/Ionization Time-of-Flight) mass spectrometry is a powerful analytical technique used for determining the mass of various molecules, particularly in biomolecular analysis, proteomics, and genomics. It utilizes a laser to ionize samples embedded in a matrix, allowing for the separation of ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio, facilitating the identification of complex biological substances.</p><p>The MALDI-TOF mass spectrometer market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for rapid and accurate microbial identification and advancements in clinical diagnostics. This growth trajectory highlights the evolving landscape of mass spectrometry and its pivotal role in various scientific fields.</p></p>
