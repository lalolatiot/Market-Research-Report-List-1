<p><p>2K Direct-to-Metal Coatings (DTMs) consist of two components that, when mixed, form a strong protective layer directly on metal surfaces. The market is divided into two types: waterborne and solventborne. Waterborne DTMs use water as a primary solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and improved environmental compliance. Solventborne DTMs, on the other hand, utilize organic solvents, offering faster drying times and robust adhesion properties, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
