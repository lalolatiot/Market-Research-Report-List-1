<p><strong>The Transcatheter Embolization Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particle embolic</li><li>Coil embolic</li><li>Liquid embolic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transcatheter Embolization Device market is segmented into three main types: particle embolics, coil embolics, and liquid embolics. Particle embolics involve small particles that occlude blood vessels, particularly effective in treating tumors. Coil embolics utilize detachable coils that promote blood clotting and vessel closure, commonly used for aneurysms. Liquid embolics consist of adhesive substances that flow and solidify in targeted areas, offering precise control in vascular occlusion. Together, these devices enhance interventional radiology treatments across various medical conditions.</p></p>
