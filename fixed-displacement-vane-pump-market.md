<p><ul><li>Single Displacement</li><li>Double Displacement</li><li>Triple Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed displacement vane pumps are categorized based on the number of displacement chambers they use. Single displacement pumps feature one chamber, providing a consistent flow rate, ideal for simple applications. Double displacement pumps utilize two chambers, allowing for greater flow capacity and improved efficiency in medium to high-pressure settings. Triple displacement pumps, with three chambers, deliver even higher flow rates and pressure capabilities, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs in hydraulic and fluid transfer systems.</p></p>
