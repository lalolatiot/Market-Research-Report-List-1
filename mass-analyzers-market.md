<p><strong>The Mass Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quadrupole Mass Analyzer</li><li>Time of Flight Mass Analyzer</li><li>Magnetic Sector Mass Analyzer</li><li>Electrostatic Sector Mass Analyzer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mass analyzers are essential in analytical chemistry, allowing for the separation and identification of ions based on their mass-to-charge ratio. The quadrupole mass analyzer uses oscillating electric fields to filter ions, commonly used in tandem with other techniques. Time-of-flight (TOF) analyzers measure the time ions take to travel a fixed distance, providing high-resolution analysis. Magnetic sector analyzers use magnetic fields for separation, while electrostatic sector analyzers utilize electric fields. Other types include ion traps and Orbitraps, offering diverse applications in research and industry.</p></p>
