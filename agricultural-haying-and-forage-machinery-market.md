<p><p>The Agricultural Haying and Forage Machinery market includes essential equipment for efficient forage production. Mowers are used to cut grass and legumes at the optimal height. Balers compact and package the cut material into manageable bales for storage. Tedders aerate and turn the forage to promote drying, ensuring quality. Rakes gather and align the dried forage into windrows for easier collection, while forage harvesters combine cutting, chopping, and loading in one operation, streamlining the harvesting process.</p></p>
