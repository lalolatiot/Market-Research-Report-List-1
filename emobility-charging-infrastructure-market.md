<p><p>The eMobility charging infrastructure market is primarily divided into two types: AC and DC charging. AC charging utilizes alternating current and typically offers slower charging speeds, making it suitable for home and workplace installations where longer charging times are acceptable. In contrast, DC charging delivers direct current, enabling much faster charging and is ideal for public charging stations, allowing vehicles to recharge quickly during long-distance travel. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the growth of electric vehicle adoption.</p></p>
