<p><p>The petroleum catalyst market comprises several key types. FCC catalysts enhance gasoline production from heavy feedstocks through fluid catalytic cracking. Hydrotreating catalysts remove impurities like sulfur from crude oil, improving its quality. Hydrocracking catalysts facilitate the breakdown of larger hydrocarbons into valuable fuels under high pressure and temperature. Catalytic reforming catalysts upgrade naphtha into high-octane gasoline. Other catalysts encompass various specialized applications that support refining processes, enabling the efficient conversion of crude oil into clean, usable fuels and chemicals.</p></p>
