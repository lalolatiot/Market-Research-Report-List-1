<p><p>Small cell networks encompass various market types based on coverage and capacity. Femtocells are low-power small cells designed for indoor use, typically serving a few users in homes or small offices. Picocells cover larger indoor areas, catering to small businesses and public venues. Microcells provide wider coverage for urban areas, acting as intermediaries between small cells and macrocells. Metrocells focus on dense urban environments, enhancing capacity and coverage for a large number of users in public spaces, addressing urban congestion challenges.</p></p>
