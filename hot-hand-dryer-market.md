<p><strong>The Hot Hand Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand-in</li><li>Hand-under</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot hand dryer market is categorized into two main types: hand-in and hand-under designs. Hand-in dryers require users to insert their hands into a designated slot, providing a more controlled airflow and reducing splatter. In contrast, hand-under dryers allow users to place their hands beneath the device, where a wide stream of hot air is directed upwards. Both types offer hygiene benefits and energy efficiency, catering to different user preferences and restroom layouts.</p></p>
