<p><strong>The Pipet Tip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips</li><li>Filtered Pipette Tips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipet tip market is primarily divided into two types: non-filtered pipette tips and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are designed for general liquid handling in laboratories, providing accuracy without additional filtration. In contrast, filtered pipette tips include a barrier that prevents contaminants and aerosolized liquids from entering the pipette, ensuring precision and minimizing cross-contamination. This distinction allows researchers to choose the appropriate tip based on their specific application needs and the level of contamination risk involved.</p></p>
