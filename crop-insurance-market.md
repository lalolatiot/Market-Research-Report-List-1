<p><strong>The Crop Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Crop Yield Insurance</li><li>Crop Price Insurance</li><li>Crop Revenue Insurance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Crop insurance comes in three primary types: Crop Yield Insurance, Crop Price Insurance, and Crop Revenue Insurance. Crop Yield Insurance protects farmers against losses due to reduced crop yields caused by adverse conditions, ensuring they receive compensation based on historical production levels. Crop Price Insurance safeguards producers from declining prices, guaranteeing a minimum price for their crops. Crop Revenue Insurance combines both yield and price protection, compensating farmers for losses in both yield and market value, thus providing a more comprehensive safety net.</p></p>
