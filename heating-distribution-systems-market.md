<p><p>Heating distribution systems encompass various technologies for residential and commercial heating. Radiators distribute heat via hot water or steam. Furnaces generate heated air for duct systems. Boilers heat water for radiators or underfloor systems. Heat pumps transfer heat from outside sources, while active solar heating utilizes solar panels to generate heat. Electric heating systems use electrical resistance to produce warmth. Other systems may include geothermal and biomass heating, catering to different efficiency and environmental needs in the market.</p></p>
