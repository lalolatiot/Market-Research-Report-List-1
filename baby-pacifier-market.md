<p><p>The Baby Pacifier market includes two main types: liquid silicone pacifiers and natural latex pacifiers. Liquid silicone pacifiers are made from a synthetic material that is durable, hypoallergenic, and easy to clean, offering a soft feel and resistance to heat. In contrast, natural latex pacifiers are crafted from rubber tree sap, providing a more natural and flexible option; however, they may not be as long-lasting as silicone. Both types cater to parents' preferences for safety, comfort, and practicality.</p></p>
