<p><ul><li>Open-tube</li><li>Sealed-tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Microfocus X-Ray Sources market comprises two primary types: open-tube and sealed-tube systems. Open-tube sources feature a design that allows for direct access to the electron beam, often providing higher resolution and flexibility in imaging applications. In contrast, sealed-tube sources are encased in a vacuum-sealed environment, offering advantages in durability and reduced maintenance needs. Both types are utilized for non-destructive testing, materials inspection, and quality control in various industrial sectors, catering to specific operational demands.</p></p>
