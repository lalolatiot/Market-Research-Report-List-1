<p><strong>The Desktop Turbidity Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scattered Light Turbidimeter</li><li>Transmitted Light Turbidimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop turbidity meters market includes two main types: scattered light turbidimeters and transmitted light turbidimeters. Scattered light turbidimeters measure the scattering of light at various angles, providing sensitive detection of low turbidity levels in samples, making them ideal for low-concentration applications. In contrast, transmitted light turbidimeters measure light passing directly through the sample, suitable for clearer water samples. Each type serves distinct needs across industries such as water quality monitoring, environmental analysis, and industrial processes.</p></p>
