<p><p>The ferro niobium market comprises two main types: standard-grade ferroniobium and vacuum-grade ferroniobium. Standard-grade ferro niobium typically contains 60-70% niobium and is primarily used in steel production to enhance strength and ductility. Vacuum-grade ferro niobium, on the other hand, has higher purity and lower contamination levels, making it suitable for high-performance applications, including aerospace and specialty alloys. Each grade serves distinct industrial sectors, influencing their market dynamics and pricing.</p></p>
