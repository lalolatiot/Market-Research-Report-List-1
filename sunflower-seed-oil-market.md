<p><p>The sunflower seed oil market is categorized into three main types: Linoleic, Mid-Oleic, and High-Oleic sunflower seed oils. Linoleic sunflower oil is rich in polyunsaturated fats, ideal for food applications requiring a light flavor. Mid-Oleic sunflower oil offers a balance of oleic and linoleic acid, providing stability and versatility. High-Oleic sunflower oil is high in monounsaturated fats, making it suitable for high-heat cooking and known for its longer shelf life, catering to health-conscious consumers and food manufacturers seeking premium oils.</p></p>
