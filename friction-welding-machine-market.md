<p><strong>The Friction Welding Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Friction Welding Machine</li><li>Linear Friction Welding Machine</li><li>Friction Stir Welding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The friction welding machine market comprises three main types. Rotary friction welding machines use rotational motion to generate heat and join materials, ideal for cylindrical parts. Linear friction welding machines involve sliding two components against each other, effective for producing strong welds in flat or irregular shapes. Friction stir welding machines utilize a non-consumable rotating tool to create a solid-state weld by softening materials without melting, making them suitable for lightweight alloys and complex geometries. Each type serves distinct applications across industries.</p></p>
