<p><p>The Neodymium magnet market is primarily divided into three types: sintered, bonded, and others. Sintered neodymium magnets are produced through a powder metallurgy process, offering high magnetic strength and temperature resistance, making them ideal for various industrial applications. Bonded neodymium magnets are formed by combining neodymium powder with a polymer binder, allowing for complex shapes and improved ductility. The 'others' category may include specialized forms or applications, catering to niche markets and specific performance requirements.</p></p>
