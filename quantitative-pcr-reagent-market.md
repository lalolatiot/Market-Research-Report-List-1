<p><strong>The Quantitative PCR Reagent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-based Reagents</li><li>Probe-based Reagents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantitative PCR reagent market comprises two main types: dye-based reagents and probe-based reagents. Dye-based reagents utilize fluorescent dyes that intercalate with the DNA during amplification, providing real-time quantification based on fluorescence intensity. In contrast, probe-based reagents employ specific probes that bind to target sequences, emitting fluorescence upon hybridization. While dye-based methods are generally simpler and cost-effective, probe-based reagents offer higher specificity and sensitivity, making them suitable for complex or multiplex assays in research and clinical applications.</p></p>
