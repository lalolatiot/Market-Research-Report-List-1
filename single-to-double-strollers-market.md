<p><ul><li>Side-by-Sides</li><li>In-Lines</li><li>Convertibles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-to-double stroller market features three primary types: side-by-sides, in-lines, and convertibles. Side-by-side strollers have two seats next to each other, providing equal access to both children but may be wider. In-line strollers, also known as tandem strollers, stack one seat behind the other, offering a more compact design for navigating tight spaces. Convertibles can transition between single and double configurations, allowing for versatile use as the family grows, making them ideal for various parenting needs.</p></p>
