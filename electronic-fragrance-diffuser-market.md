<p><strong>The Electronic Fragrance Diffuser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Diffuser</li><li>Evaporation Diffuser</li><li>Electrothermal Diffuser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic fragrance diffuser market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Ultrasonic Diffuser**: Utilizes ultrasonic vibrations to break essential oils into fine mist, dispersing fragrance and adding moisture to the air.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Evaporation Diffuser**: Relies on natural airflow to disperse fragrance from the surface of oils, typically using fans or passive diffusion methods.</p><p>3. **Electrothermal Diffuser**: Heats essential oils to release their scent into the air, providing a quick and intense aroma but often with a shorter duration. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and needs.</p></p>
