<p><p>The radar market comprises several types, each serving distinct functions. Bistatic radar uses separate transmitter and receiver locations, enhancing detection capabilities. Continuous-wave radar emits constant signals for precise speed measurement. Doppler radar leverages frequency shifts to determine object velocity. FM-CW radar combines frequency modulation with continuous wave for improved range resolution. Monopulse radar rapidly acquires targets by comparing multiple signals simultaneously. Together, these radar types cater to various applications, including aviation, automotive, and military, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
