<p><p>Halogen-free cored solder wire comes in three main types: No Clean, Water Soluble, and Rosin Based. No Clean solder uses minimal flux, leaving little to no residue, making it ideal for delicate electronics where cleaning is impractical. Water Soluble solder requires cleaning after use, as it leaves a residue that can corrode components if left unchecked. Rosin Based solder combines traditional rosin with halogen-free flux, offering reliability and ease of use, while maintaining environmental standards. Each type caters to specific soldering requirements.</p></p>
