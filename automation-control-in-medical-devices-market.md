<p><p>Automation control in the medical devices market encompasses various systems, each serving specific functions. MES (Manufacturing Execution Systems) optimize production processes by tracking work-in-progress and managing workflows. PLC (Programmable Logic Controllers) automate equipment control and ensure processes run smoothly. DCS (Distributed Control Systems) manage complex processes by integrating multiple control loops for real-time monitoring. SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) provides centralized control and data collection for large-scale operations, enhancing efficiency and compliance in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
