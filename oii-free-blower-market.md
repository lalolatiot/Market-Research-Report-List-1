<p><p>The Oil Free Blower market is categorized into three types based on pressure levels: Low Pressure Blowers, Medium Pressure Blowers, and High Pressure Blowers. Low Pressure Blowers operate at pressures below 0.5 bar and are suitable for applications like aeration in wastewater treatment. Medium Pressure Blowers, ranging from 0.5 to 1.5 bar, find use in pneumatic conveying and industrial processes. High Pressure Blowers exceed 1.5 bar, serving demanding applications like vacuum systems and refrigeration, ensuring efficiency and reliability without oil contamination.</p></p>
