<p><p>A Reactive Load Bank is a crucial device used to simulate various electrical loads for testing and maintaining power generation equipment, particularly generators and uninterruptible power supplies. By providing a reliable load when actual loads are absent, these systems ensure that equipment operates efficiently and reliably under load conditions. <p><p>The Reactive Load Bank market consists of three primary types: portable, trailer-mounted, and stationary. Trailer-mounted load banks combine mobility with a robust design, offering flexibility for larger sites and scenarios needing significant power testing. Stationary load banks, on the other hand, are fixed installations, typically used in permanent facilities for consistent testing and maintenance of power systems.</p></p>
