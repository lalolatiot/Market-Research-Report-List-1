<p><p>The fertilizer tester market is categorized into two main types: liquid and dry fertilizer testers. Liquid fertilizer testers are designed to analyze the nutrient content and pH levels of liquid fertilizers, ensuring optimal application for crops. They often provide real-time data, making them suitable for precision agriculture. Dry fertilizer testers, on the other hand, assess granular fertilizers, focusing on nutrient distribution and soil compatibility. Both types are essential for maximizing agricultural productivity and ensuring efficient fertilizer use.</p></p>
