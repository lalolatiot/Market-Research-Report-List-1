<p><p>The Vital Signs Monitor market consists of different types including in-ear and surface-mount device (SMD) monitors. SMD monitors, on the other hand, are integrated into various medical devices and provide real-time vital sign tracking through compact electronic components. Both types aim to enhance patient care and streamline data collection in clinical settings.</p></p>
