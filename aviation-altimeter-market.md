<p><strong>The Aviation Altimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barometric Altimeter</li><li>Radio Altimeter</li><li>GNSS (GPS, Galileo, etc).</li><li>Laser Altimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation altimeter market comprises several types: Barometric altimeters measure altitude based on atmospheric pressure changes, making them essential for pilot navigation and altitude reporting. Radio altimeters use radar waves to determine the aircraft's height above ground level, providing precise measurements during landing. GNSS altimeters leverage satellite signals, like GPS or Galileo, for accurate positioning and altitude data. Laser altimeters utilize laser technology for altitude measurement, offering high precision for both airborne and ground applications. Each type serves distinct roles in aviation safety and navigation.</p></p>
