<p><p>The Transportation and Logistics Software market is primarily classified into two types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based solutions. In contrast, Cloud-Based solutions are hosted on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier updates. They typically offer a subscription model, enabling businesses to access the software from anywhere while reducing infrastructure costs. Both types serve distinct operational needs within the logistics sector.</p></p>
