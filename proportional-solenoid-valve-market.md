<p><p>The proportional solenoid valve market includes three main types: pressure control valves, flow control valves, and directional control valves. Pressure control valves regulate system pressure by adjusting the flow of fluid in response to pressure changes. Flow control valves manage the rate of fluid flow, ensuring optimal performance in hydraulic and pneumatic systems. Directional control valves control the path of fluid, enabling precise movement and operation of machinery. Together, these valves enhance system efficiency, accuracy, and responsiveness in various applications.</p></p>
