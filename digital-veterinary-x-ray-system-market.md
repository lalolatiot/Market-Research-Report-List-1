<p><strong>The Digital Veterinary X-ray System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Computed Radiography</li><li>Direct Radiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Veterinary X-ray System market is primarily segmented into two types: Computed Radiography (CR) and Direct Radiography (DR). Computed Radiography employs a phosphor plate to capture images, which are then digitized using a scanner, making it versatile and cost-effective. In contrast, Direct Radiography utilizes flat-panel detectors to capture images directly, providing faster results and higher image quality. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities in veterinary practices, enabling more accurate assessments of animal health.</p></p>
