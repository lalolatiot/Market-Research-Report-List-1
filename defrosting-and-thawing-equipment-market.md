<p><p>The defrosting and thawing equipment market comprises several types, including chamber type and rotary type. Chamber type equipment uses enclosed spaces to control temperature and humidity, facilitating even thawing without compromising food quality. In contrast, rotary type equipment employs a rotational mechanism to enhance heat distribution, enabling faster and more uniform thawing. Both types cater to various industries, including food processing and catering, emphasizing efficiency, safety, and quality in thawing frozen products.</p></p>
