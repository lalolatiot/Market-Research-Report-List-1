<p><strong>The Automatic Car Wash Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gantry Car Wash</li><li>Conveyor Tunnel System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic car wash equipment market primarily includes two main types: Gantry car wash systems and conveyor tunnel systems. Gantry car washes utilize overhead frames that move along a track, providing a thorough wash as vehicles remain stationary. These systems are ideal for smaller spaces and often cater to self-service models. In contrast, conveyor tunnel systems involve vehicles being driven onto a conveyor belt, allowing for a continuous flow of cars through various wash stages, making them efficient for high-volume operations.</p></p>
