<p><p>The silicone building sealant market is divided into one-component and two-component types. One-component sealants are pre-mixed, ready-to-use products that cure upon exposure to moisture, making them convenient for quick applications. They are widely used in residential and commercial construction for sealing joints and gaps. Two-component sealants require mixing before application and offer faster curing and enhanced performance, ideal for industrial or large-scale projects. Both types provide durability, weather resistance, and flexibility, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
