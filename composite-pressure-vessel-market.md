<p><p>The composite pressure vessel market can be categorized primarily into Type III and Type IV vessels, along with other variants. Type III vessels are constructed with a metal liner combined with composite materials, offering a balance of strength and weight, geared towards high-pressure applications. Type IV vessels, on the other hand, utilize a polymer liner encased in composite materials, allowing for lighter designs and improved resistance to chemicals. Other types may include hybrid designs or specialized configurations tailored to specific industries and applications.</p></p>
