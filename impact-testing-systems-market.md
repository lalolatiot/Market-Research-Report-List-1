<p><p>Impact testing systems are categorized into various types, primarily pendulum testing and drop weight testing. Pendulum testing involves swinging a pendulum to strike a specimen, simulating impact forces and measuring material resilience. Drop weight testing, on the other hand, involves dropping a weight from a predetermined height onto a sample, assessing its ability to absorb energy and withstand sudden forces. Both methods are crucial for evaluating material toughness and durability for applications in engineering and manufacturing, ensuring safety and reliability.</p></p>
