<p><p>A Single Phase Vacuum Circuit Breaker (SPVCB) is a type of electrical switchgear designed to protect electrical systems by interrupting the current flow during fault conditions. SPVCBs are commonly employed in medium voltage applications and are advantageous for their compact size, robustness, and enhanced safety features.</p><p>The Single Phase Vacuum Circuit Breaker Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.7% during the forecast period.
