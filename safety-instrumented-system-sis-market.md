<p><p>A Safety Instrumented System (SIS) is designed to prevent or mitigate hazardous events in industrial processes. The market segments include sensors, which detect process parameters and potential failures; logic solvers, which analyze sensor inputs and execute safety functions; and final elements, which implement the corrective actions, such as valves or actuators. Together, these components work in tandem to ensure process safety, reduce risks, and comply with industry safety standards, thus playing a critical role in operational reliability.</p></p>
