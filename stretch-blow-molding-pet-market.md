<p><strong>The Stretch Blow Molding PET Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Process</li><li>Two-Stage Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stretch blow molding of PET involves two primary processes: single-stage and two-stage. In the single-stage process, preform production and bottle blowing occur simultaneously in one machine, allowing for efficient production and reduced costs. Conversely, the two-stage process separates preform creation and bottle stretching, which offers greater design flexibility and higher production quality. Each method serves different market needs, with single-stage favored for high-volume, simple designs, and two-stage preferred for complex or specialty containers.</p></p>
