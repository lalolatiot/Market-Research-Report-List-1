<p><p>The modular commercial building market is categorized into two primary types: permanent and relocatable structures. Permanent modular buildings are designed for long-term use, often featuring durable materials and integrated systems, making them suitable for schools, offices, and healthcare facilities. In contrast, relocatable modular buildings offer flexibility, allowing for easy transport and reinstallation in different locations, catering to temporary needs like construction sites or seasonal events. Both types streamline construction timelines and enhance cost efficiency in diverse settings.</p></p>
